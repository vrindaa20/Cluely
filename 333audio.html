Case #103  ·  Serial Homicide — Domestic

The Quiet House

Solved
Case Summary

Three deaths. One household. Eleven months apart. When Suresh Kapoor's elderly mother died from a staircase fall, it was filed as an accident. When his younger brother passed six months later from accidental rat poison ingestion, the family doctor quietly flagged it but didn't push. When Suresh's wife stopped breathing in her sleep and was declared a cardiac event, a cousin finally said enough and came to us. Each death had one constant: Suresh was the sole person present. And after each, he stood to inherit. We didn't begin with a theory. We began with the insurance policies.

Week 2Independent forensic toxicologist reviews Meena's post-mortem. Flags elevated KCl — inconsistent with natural cardiac death.
Week 3Hardware store CCTV confirms Suresh purchased rat poison — same brand as Dinesh's case — 11 days before Dinesh died.

Week 6Full evidence handed to Haryana CID. Suresh Kapoor arrested at his office in Panchkula. Did not resist.
