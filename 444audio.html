Case #104  ·  Missing Persons

The Vanishing Bride

Unsolved — Investigation Ongoing
Case Summary

The baraat had arrived. The pheras were set to begin at 7 PM. At 6:42 PM, Nikita Sharma, 24, told her closest friend she needed a moment alone and stepped into the adjoining room of the banquet hall. She never came back out. When family went to check on her eighteen minutes later, the room was empty. Her lehenga was draped neatly over the chair. Her phone was left behind — screen cracked, last message sent at 6:38 PM. The groom's family demanded answers. The girl's family was in shock. Her parents hired us that same night. There was no forced exit. No CCTV of her leaving the building. Nikita disappeared from inside a locked room, during her own wedding, without a trace — and nobody we've interviewed since has told us the whole truth.
